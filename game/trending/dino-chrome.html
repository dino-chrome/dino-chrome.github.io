Discover the World of Dino Chrome: Your Dinosaur Game Destination

Welcome to Dino Chrome, the ultimate hub for all things related to the iconic Dinosaur Game. Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer, we've got everything you need to enjoy this timeless classic.

What is Dino Chrome?

Dino Chrome is a dedicated website that provides easy access to the beloved Dinosaur Game, also known as the Chrome Dinosaur or T-Rex Game. We offer a seamless experience, allowing you to play the original game and discover exciting variations and mods.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform where players can enjoy the simple yet addictive gameplay of the Dinosaur Game without any hassle. We're passionate about preserving and celebrating this classic browser game.

Why Play the Dinosaur Game on Dino Chrome?

  • Community: Connect with other Dinosaur Game enthusiasts and share your high scores.

How to Play the Dinosaur Game on Dino Chrome

Playing the Dinosaur Game on Dino Chrome is simple:

  1. Visit Dino Chrome in your web browser.
  2. The Dinosaur Game will load automatically.
  3. Press the Spacebar (or tap on mobile) to start the game.
  4. Use the Spacebar or Up Arrow to jump and the Down Arrow to duck.
  5. Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!
